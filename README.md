TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION:
Platform-agnostic, single player retro arcade survival game, featuring top-down, 2D perspective. Utilizes custom shaders, UI, collision, and movement systems.

MARKETING DESCRIPTION:
Pilot your humble spaceship across the cosmos, blasting baddies and dodging debris all the way to galactic stardom!

CONTROLS:
Use mouse or touchscreen to navigate menus. The game is played with two buttons. The left button fires your weapon, while holding or releasing the right button toggles between clockwise and counterclockwise movement, effectively allowing you to "steer" anywhere in the top-down coordinate plane. On a touchscreen, the "left button" means pressing anywhere on the left half of the screen, while the "right button" is anywhere on the right half of the screen. On a keyboard, the left button is the ~ key, and the right button is the 1 key.
